WELCOME TO ELESA !

Integrated with Integrity...!!

Electronics Engineering Students Association is one of the technical club in Walchand College Of Engineering, Sangli. The club is by the students, of the students and for the students under the guidance of HOD, Staff Advisors and some teaching staff. Currently ELESA has 60 ongoing members divided into multiple posts working unitedly!
